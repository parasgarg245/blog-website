Tips From our Happiness Consultants to Pick the Right Gifts for Birthday

Picking the right birthday gift is a science, but definitely not a rocket science. It’s just a gifting science! The best way to decide on a b’day gift is to know your budget and who you want to gift. The budget – decide how much you can spend based on your financial situation. Just don’t put your entire month’s salary into one gift (unless you’ve forgotten her birthday, in which case you’ll need 3 months’ salary for make-up gifting). Once you know that, all you need is to know a little bit about the person you want to surprise with a wonderful birthday gift! Is he stupidly romantic who has seen every Shah Rukh movie? Is she a travel freak who wants to (or has already) travelled the world? Is he an animal lover who’d rather have dogs than children? Is she a selfie lover, who can’t stop obsessing over her photos? Is he a prankster who needs to be given a dose of his own medicine? Is she someone who has everything and would rather contribute to make the world a better place?
